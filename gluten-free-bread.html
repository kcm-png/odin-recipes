Gluten Free Bread

Description

This recipe provides a simple and delicious way to bake your own gluten-free bread at home. The result is a soft, flavorful loaf with a wonderful crust, perfect for sandwiches, toast, or enjoying on its own. It's designed to be straightforward, even for those new to gluten-free baking.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose gluten-free flour blend
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) instant yeast
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (about 110째F or 43째C)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the warm water, olive oil, and eggs.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix with a spoon or spatula until just combined. The dough will be sticky.
  4. Transfer the dough to a greased 9x5 inch loaf pan and smooth the top.
  5. Cover the pan with a clean towel and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  6. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
