Gluten Free Dessert

Description

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this incredible gluten-free dessert recipe. It's rich, decadent, and surprisingly easy to prepare. Whether you're making it for a special occasion or just as a treat, this dessert is sure to impress everyone, regardless of their dietary needs.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup hot water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  5. Carefully pour the hot water into the batter and whisk until well combined. The batter will be thin.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool in the pan for 15 minutes before inverting it onto a wire rack to cool completely.
